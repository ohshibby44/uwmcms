Open to all Boeing members and their families enrolled in the Preferred Partnership option in 2019, Mental Health CareConnect provides free, confidential consultation for behavioral and mental health conditions and concerns. Our trained Patient Navigator will connect you via phone to a psychiatrist or psychologist within one business day, and often the same day.
